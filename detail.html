Vegetarian Tacos

4.3 stars

Ingredients

Amount Food Item
1 Cup Green Beans
2 Ears Fresh Corn
2 cups Tomatillo Salsa
8 corn tortillas
0.5 cups Cotija Cheese

Steps

  1. Blanch the green beans
  2. Grill the green beans and the ears of the corn
  3. Chop the green beans
  4. Slice the corn of the cob
  5. Grill the tortillas
  6. Assemble the tacos with veggies, salsa, and cheese
